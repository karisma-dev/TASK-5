About Puma

History of Puma

Puma is a German multinational corporation that designs and manufactures athletic and casual footwear, apparel, and accessories. The company was founded in 1948 by Rudolf Dassler, the brother of Adidas founder Adolf Dassler.

Puma Products

Puma offers a wide range of products, including sports shoes, lifestyle footwear, activewear, and accessories. Known for its innovative designs and performance-oriented products, Puma has become a popular choice among athletes and fashion enthusiasts alike.

Global Presence

With a global presence, Puma has established itself as a leading brand in the sports and lifestyle industry. The company sponsors various sports teams and athletes worldwide, contributing to its recognition and influence in the market.
